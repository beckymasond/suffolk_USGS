Data Source: 2010-2014 American Community Survey 5-Year Summary File
 
Downloaded via the Census Bureau's API: http://www.census.gov/data/developers/data-sets/acs-survey-5-year-data.html

Files:

blockgroup_occupancy_ests.csv - List of block group variable estimates
blockgroup_occupancy_moes.csv - List of block group variable margins of error (MOE) associated with estimates
blockgroup_occupancy_columns.csv - Definitions for column names in ests and moes files
